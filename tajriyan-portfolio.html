The Designer

Tajriyan
Tasfiq

"I believe great design is not decoration — it is the architecture of perception."

Based in Dhaka, I am a visual designer specializing in brand identity, logo creation, and packaging design. With over five years of craft-driven practice, I work with founders, studios, and businesses who understand that how something looks determines how it is remembered.

My approach blends conceptual rigour with meticulous execution. I research deeply, sketch obsessively, and refine endlessly — until the work feels inevitable.

Offerings

Services &
Specializations

01
Logo &
Mark Design
The foundation of every brand. I design logomarks, wordmarks, and combination marks that are strategically considered and endlessly versatile — built to work across every scale and context. Each mark is developed through a rigorous process of research, sketching, and digital refinement.
→
02
Brand Identity
Systems
Beyond the logo — complete visual systems that include color palettes, typography hierarchies, graphic elements, iconography, photography direction, and comprehensive brand guidelines. I create living design systems that scale from a business card to a billboard.
→
03
Packaging
Design
Packaging is the product's first handshake. I design retail, luxury, and e-commerce packaging that communicates quality through material consideration, structural elegance, and precise visual execution. From single SKUs to full product family systems.
→
04
Brand Collateral
& Print
Business cards, stationery, signage, brochures, lookbooks, and all supporting brand materials. Every touchpoint is an opportunity to reinforce brand perception — designed with the same attention to detail as the core identity.
→
05
Brand Strategy
& Positioning
Design without strategy is decoration. I offer brand positioning workshops and strategic foundations — defining brand purpose, personality, voice, and competitive differentiation — before a single pixel is touched.
→
06
Visual Art
Direction
For campaigns, launches, and brand activations — comprehensive art direction covering photography style, color grading, layout systems, and visual narrative. Ensuring every touchpoint tells a cohesive story with maximum visual impact.
